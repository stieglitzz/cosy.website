Tiny Awards

A celebration of the small, personal & whimsical web. All the sites nominated are wonderful but if I have to pick some favourites...

  • Diagram Website

    A collection within a collection. A giant venn diagram. A different way to present a directory.

  • WeExpire

    Write a note for someone and they will receive it "after your death or if you are seriously injured". I don't know who I'd write my death note to or what I would.

    One day I will write more about death and digital legacy. It's now something you have to consider leading up to your death, just like a will. Who will inherit your physical and digital objects?
