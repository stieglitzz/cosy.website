I now spell the word 'colour' without the 'u'.

In Australia (where I live), the word colour is spelt with a 'u'. Until last year, I spelt it with a 'u'. When writing CSS, the word colour does not have a 'u'. This because a mild annoyance for me so I dropped the 'u'. I like it better. The word is more symmetrical without the 'u'.

Anyway, this is a collection of colours I like. Some of them are grouped together as palettes.
